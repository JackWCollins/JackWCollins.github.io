As a technology leader and software engineer with experience in the startup world, I, Jack Collins, am dedicated to supporting my team and the organizations I work with. Over the past 3.5 years, I have served as the CTO and co-founder of Gondola, where I've focused on leveraging my technical expertise and communication skills to contribute to the company's success.
Before joining Gondola, I was the first non-founder engineer at Galore. During my time there, I worked collaboratively with my colleagues to scale the startup for two years until it was acquired by Care.com.
I believe in servant leadership and strive to foster a positive and inclusive company culture. My approach involves listening to my team, guiding them through challenges, and setting high-level strategic direction that supports the overall growth of the organization. As a software engineer with a focus on effective communication, I am committed to contributing my skills and experience to any technology-driven organization while prioritizing the growth and well-being of my team.
